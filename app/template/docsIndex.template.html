Welcome to the Disco Docs

You will be happy to hear that Disco uses composer for dependency management and installation.

Where to get started?

First we recommend that you head over to the installation page and get a working copy of Disco up and running

Then check out configuring your local and development environments at the configuration page

After that head over to learn about the Request Lifecycle

Then take a quick read of the core principles empowering Disco; Inversion of Control and Facades

Now get to the good stuff and learn how to RESTfully route your application and leverage Controllers

Then get familiar with the View Facade and the tDisco Templating System

Disco is built around some basic principles:

  • KISS
  • Reusability
  • Modularity
  • RESTful routing
  • Portability
  • IoC and Facades
